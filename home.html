Series of the Month

Smiskis

These green human figures push the limits of traditional collectibles. While other collectibles have more intricate designs, these figures are more on the simple side. Their faces all display a simple emotionless face and are pasted onto each figurine. The vibrant green color creates a sense of uniqueness compared to other figures. The figurines are also not to mention glow in the dark. Placing under a light will easily charge the figurines and they will glow bright as ever in the dark.. Their simplicity allows the viewers to take appreciation in the minimalistic designs and pay more attention to their actions and poses rather than extreme detail. The overall aesthetic successfully balances modern minimalism with a playful, almost surreal quality, making them popular among those who value originality and simplicity.

Sony Angels

Sony angels known for their cute designs are a series of baby figures dressed in a veriety of different outfits whether they are animal costumes, desserts and more. They will always guarantee a cute design!

Pino Jelly

The "Pino Jelly" series is one of Pop Mart's most endearing lines, distinguished by its playful and cutesy aesthetic. Each figure in this series has soft, rounded forms and vibrant colors that evoke joy and whimsy. The characters' jelly-like look gives them a dynamic, almost liquid appearance that is both visually appealing and conceptually refreshing. Pino Jelly overall figures are similar to one another however the colors and accessories really make them unique. The accessible design of "Pino Jelly" makes it an ideal entry point for those new to the world of blind box collectibles, while also appealing to seasoned collectors who appreciate its charming aesthetics.
