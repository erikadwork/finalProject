Sample Figure Collection Map

This interactive map showcases one of our personal collections—each pinpoint marks a favorite figurine or collectible we’ve discovered over time. By overlaying our personal story in this format, we hope to encourage you on your own collecting passion! From real Smiskis and fan-made Smiski stickers to special collabs like McDonald’s × Minecraft and Haikyu! keychains, every piece represents a unique find and the excitement of the hunt. We hope this inspires fellow enthusiasts to chart their own collections, share their discoveries, and celebrate the joy of gathering those special pieces that make collecting so rewarding.

Sonny Angel

A family friend gifted them this Sonny Angel in a birthday-cake costume for their birthday—it was their very first in the collection. With its soft pastel palette and charming details, it immediately stole the spotlight.

Smiskis

These Smiskis come as a matching pair of mini figures, scored in a sweet trade with a friend. They stack perfectly on top of each other, and in their eyes, it was absolutely worth the swap.

McDonald's × Minecraft Figurine

Once they heard about the Minecraft × McDonald’s mystery-box collab, they couldn’t resist diving in. Laughter and excited chatter filled the room as each box was unwrapped, each reveal bringing a sweet rush of childhood nostalgia.

Lulu Figurine

Exploring the newly opened store, they spotted a mystery box themed after one of their favorite games. When they lifted the lid to find their main character inside, they couldn’t contain their excitement—it was exactly the one they’d been hoping for.

Haikyu!! Keychain

Stumbling across a keychain set from one of their favorite animated series, they couldn’t resist grabbing one. When they finally opened it, they found their go-to character sporting a hilariously unexpected expression—an instant laugh that brightened their day.

Smiski Stickers

They spotted these custom Smiski stickers at a campus artist alley and, being huge fans of the series, couldn’t walk away. When they peeled back the mystery pack, they were over the moon with how cute and unique each design turned out.

Haikyu!! Figurine

Caught up in the excitement of one of their favorite shows, they snagged another mystery box—partly because a friend picked it out, fingers crossed for the perfect character. Sure enough, luck was on their side, and they unboxed exactly who they’d been hoping for.
