Sample Figure Collection Map

This interactive map showcases one of our personal collections—each pinpoint marks a favorite figurine or collectible we’ve discovered over time. By overlaying our personal story in this format, we hope to encourage you on your own collecting passion! From real Smiskis and fan-made Smiski stickers to special collabs like McDonald’s × Minecraft and Haikyu! keychains, every piece represents a unique find and the excitement of the hunt. We hope this inspires fellow enthusiasts to chart their own collections, share their discoveries, and celebrate the joy of gathering those special pieces that make collecting so rewarding.
